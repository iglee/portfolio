Outrun Jules Verne

Outrun Jules Verne is a data science project to personalize travels. You get to pick features based on your interests, sentiment, and whim, and I get to recommend you a route around the globe. Specifically, this model uses a fun combination of Natural Language Processing on Spark for feature extraction and Monte Carlo Metropolis for some randomness.
Technology used: Python, Spark, Hadoop, AWS, and Bootstrap - Greyscale.
